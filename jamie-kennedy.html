Jamie Kennedy News - Us Weekly

Name:Jamie Kennedy

Hometown:Upper Darby, PA

Relationship:Single

Birthday:May 25, 1970

About

Jamie Kennedy was born in Upper Darby, Penn., on May 25, 1970. Most famously known for his own show, The Jamie Kennedy Experiment, he has entertained many people with his hidden cameras and pranks.

He is a multi-talented comedian, actor, writer and producer. In the summer of 2003, Kennedy conquered the book market with the release of his autobiography Wannabe…A Hollywood Experiment. He has also starred in three of the Scream movies and written and produced many different TV shows including Living with Fran, starring Fran Drescher.

Romantically, Kennedy has been linked to Paris Hilton, Mena Suvari and Jennifer Love Hewitt. He currently tours the country doing stand-up comedy and lives in LA.
