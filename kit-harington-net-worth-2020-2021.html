Early Life

Christopher Catesby Harington was born on December 26, 1986, in Acton, London, United Kingdom. His father, David Richard Harington, is a businessman, and his mother, Deborah Jane Catesby, used to work as a dramatist. Kit Harington has a brother, and his name is John Harington.

Kit Harington studied at The Chantry School, where he moved along with his family, in Worcestershire. Then, he studied drama and theater at Worcester Sixth Form College, and in 2008, he graduated from the Royal Central School of Speech & Drama.

In 2017, Kit Harington got engaged to actress Rose Leslie.
