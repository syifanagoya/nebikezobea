The Journey of a Stand-Up Comedian to SNL Stardom

She is a name synonymous with vibrant comedy and impeccable impressions, embarked on her journey in the world of laughter in Whittier, California.

Born to Mexican parents, Melissa’s rich cultural heritage has often been a wellspring of inspiration in her acts.

Her comedic flair was evident early on, leading her to the stages of the Laugh Factory Comedy Camp at just 15, a move that set the stage for a flourishing career.

Melissa’s talent shone brightly as she ventured into television, becoming a seminal figure on NBC’s Saturday Night Live (SNL) in 2016.
