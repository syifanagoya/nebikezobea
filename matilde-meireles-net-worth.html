FAQs about Matilde Meireles

Who is Matilde Meireles?

She is a Portuguese field recordist, sound artist, and researcher. She has a PhD in Sonic Arts and is known for her work with site-specific audio and visual installations.

What kind of music does she produce?

Meireles specializes in field recordings, turning everyday sounds into immersive audio experiences. Her work often involves experimental and drone music, as well as conceptual art.

What are some notable projects by Matilde Meireles?

Some of her significant works include Life of a Potato, Sunnyside, Useful Constraints, and MILL~MMXXI.
