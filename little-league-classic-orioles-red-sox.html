2022 Little League Classic: Orioles beat Red Sox in Williamsport

The Baltimore Orioles defeated the Boston Red Sox 5-3 in the 2022 Little League Classic in Williamsport, Pa., on Sunday.

Orioles starter Dean Kremer threw 5 1/3 innings, allowing one run on five hits with two walks, striking out four. Red Sox starter Nick Pivetta tossed 5 2/3 innings, giving up one run on six hits and striking out nine batters.
