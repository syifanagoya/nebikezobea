Guillermo Ochoa

Memo is a nickname for Guillermo Ochoa, a Mexican footballer. He is presently the goalie for Liga MX club America and the Mexican national team.

Details on Guillermo Ochoa net worth and career

Guillermo Ochoa, 35, is expected to have a net worth of roughly $7 million by 2020. In 2014, his annual income was projected to be over $2.275 million. As a result, he consistently earns a salary in the same range.

CAPTION: Guillermo Ochoa is a Mexican professional footballer.(SOURCE: World Footba)

Guillermo began his professional football career with America at the age of 18 years old. From 2003 to 2011, he was a member of the team. He has played in 263 matches in all, winning Golden Glove honors and been named to France Football’s list of candidates for the Ballon d’Or in the process.

He was transferred to Ajaccio on a three-year contract in the 2011-2012 season. He was worth $8.80 million in the 2011-12 season. He was named Player of the Season in both his first and second seasons. He signed a three-year contract with Malaga in the Spanish La Liga for $5.5 million for the 2014-2015 season. In 2014, there were rumors that Liverpool would make a £4 million approach for the player. The International Federation of Football History and Statistics placed him eighth among the world’s top goalkeepers in 2014.

He was placed on a season-long loan to Granada for the 2016 season. He was selected Player of the Season by the club’s fans throughout the year. In 2017, he was traded to Standard Liege, a Belgian club. He was selected Player of the Season for the second year in a row in 2018.

Guillermo returned to Club America in 2019 on a three-and-a-half-year contract. He was the highest-paid Mexican footballer in the Liga MX, earning $4.4 million per year, according to reports.

Guillermo has also represented his hometown. In the 2006 FIFA World Cup, he was the third-choice goalkeeper. He was also a backup goalkeeper in the 2010 World Cup qualifiers. He was, nevertheless, the first-choice goalie for the 2014 and 2018 World Cups.

Guillermo Ochoa’s biographical sketch

Guillermo Ochoa was born Francisco Guillermo Ochoa Magana in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico, on July 13, 1985. His nationality is Mexican, and his ethnicity is Hispanic. He received his schooling from the Johan Cruyff Institute’s online Football Management Degree program.

Is Guillermo Ochoa married or in a relationship?

Guillermo Ochoa, who stands 6 feet 1 inch tall, is married. On July 8, 2017, in Ibiza, Spain, he married the woman of his dreams, Karla Mora, a Mexican model. Following his departure to France, the two began dating.

CAPTION: Guillermo Ochoa is married and has three kids.(SOURCE: Instagram)

Guillermo and his partner, Karla, received their first child, a daughter, Lucciana, on February 8, 2013, in Corsica, after dating for a few years. On April 1, 2015, he had his second kid, a son, two years later. In 2017, the couple married, and two years later, in May 2019, they had their second daughter. Prior to marrying his present wife, Ochoa was in a relationship with Dulce Maria, a Mexican actress and singer. From 2005 to 2006, they only dated for a year.
