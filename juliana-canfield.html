“The Calling” Actress Juliana Canfield Wiki | Bio

Juliana Canfield (born on February 18, 1992, age: 30) is an American actress who starred in multiple hit movies like “The Assistant,” “On the Rocks” and “Plain Fiction.” Besides films, she also featured in TV shows, including “Amazing Stories” and “Succession.”

In 2022, Juliana catapulted into fame after she starred alongside Jeff Wilbusch in Barry Levinson’s “The Calling,” which landed on Peacock on November 10. It is a crime drama, the plot of which spins around an NYPD detective named Avi (played by Jeff Wilbusch) who tries to solve a murder mystery.

According to Juliana’s IMDB profile, she is currently working on season 4 of the HBO series “Succession,” which is expected to be released in 2023. Besides “Succession,” she is also working with Tony Gerber on a movie titled “Everlasting Yea!” that also includes Jovan Adepo, the actor who played Cory in “Fences.”
