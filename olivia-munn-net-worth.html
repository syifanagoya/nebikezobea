Olivia Munn Net Worth - NewsNow Nigeria

Olivia Munn, born on 3 July 1980 in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma USA, has built a net worth of $15 million through her career in the entertainment industry. Munn’s multicultural background (Vietnamese, British, and German ancestry) has contributed to her success in various roles as a television personality, presenter, actress, model, and author.

Munn spent a significant part of her childhood in Tokyo, Japan, and began her career as a model. She later pursued journalism at the University of Oklahoma before venturing into acting. Munn’s early on-screen appearances included roles in “Attack of the Show!” and films such as “Big Stan” and “Iron Man 2”.

In addition to her acting career, Munn has achieved success as a model, endorsing brands like Neutrogena, Pepsi, and Nike. She has also appeared on the covers of notable magazines such as Playboy and “Vanity Fair”.

Munn is known for her role as Psylocke in “X-Men: Apocalypse” and has contributed to the animated series “Miles from Tomorrowland”. Her book, “Suck It, Wonder Woman: The Misadventures of a Hollywood Geek”, also added to her net worth.

In her personal life, Munn has been in relationships with actor Joel Kinnaman and NFL quarterback Aaron Rodgers. She is a dedicated supporter of PETA’s anti-animal cruelty campaigns.

Munn is a multi-talented individual with a diverse skill set which has greatly contributed to her financial success.
