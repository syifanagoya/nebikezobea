Bridget Hall

Bridget Hall – Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Spring 2008 – Calvin Klein Underwear, 2007 – Photo Credit: Janet Mayer / PR Photos

Birth Name: Bridget Lee Hall

Place of Birth: Springdale, Arkansas, U.S.

Date of Birth: December 12, 1977

Ethnicity: English, German, Scottish, Irish, Welsh

Bridget Hall is an American model. She has appeared on the cover of Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, ELLE, and Allure; modeled for Pepsi, Guess Jeans, and Anne Klein; and walked the runway in fashion shows in New York City, Paris, and Milan. She was included in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue from 2002 to 2005.

She is the daughter of Donna and Roger Hall.

Bridget’s maternal grandfather was Van Isaac Petty (the son of Van Albert Petty and Laura Gertrude Stell). Van Isaac was born in Arkansas. Van Albert was born in Missouri, the son of Charles Albert Petty and Sarah Frances Miller. Laura was born in Arkansas, the daughter of James Carroll Stell and Della Francis Haley.

Bridget’s maternal grandmother was Alice Zelma Kumpe (the daughter of David Franklin Kumpe and Mary Ester Emmaline Pritchard). Alice was born in Arkansas. David was born in Arkansas, the son of John Otto Kumpe, Jr., whose father was German, and of Mary Oney Crutchfield. Bridget’s great-grandmother Mary Ester was born in Arkansas, the daughter of John Franklin Pritchard and Martha Elizabeth Orick.
